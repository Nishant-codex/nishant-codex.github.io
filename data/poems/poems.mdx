---
title: Whispers of the Night
date: '2024-12-28'
tags: ['Reflections', 'Nature']
summary: In the silence of the evening, thoughts drift like clouds across the sky
---

In the silence of the evening,
When the world begins to sleep,
Whispers float upon the darkness,
Secrets that the night will keep.

Stars emerge like distant memories,
Scattered across the endless sky,
Each one telling ancient stories,
Of moments passing gently by.

The world grows still and contemplative,
As shadows dance and slowly creep,
And in this hour of solitude,
The soul finds promises to keep.
